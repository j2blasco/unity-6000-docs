### Description
The size of the area light.
Rect lights: size of Area Light's rectangle. Disc lights: .x is the radius of the Disc light. Tube lights: .x is the length of the Tube light. Pyramid lights: .x is the aspect ratio. Additional resources: [Light component](https://docs.unity3d.com/6000.0/Documentation/Manual/class-Light.html).
